Open both sample2.html and sample2.css in your text editor, and play around with both, editing and saving to see what happens. Generally, when you change the CSS, you have to "force-reload" to prevent your browser from just using its cached copy of sample2.css. Sometimes you have to force-reload multiple times until the browser gets the hint. This is super-annoying. For most browsers, you can force-reload via Shift-Ctrl-R (Windows and Linux) or Shift-Command-R (Mac).
